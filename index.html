Sourcer is a human centered technology talent acquisition consultancy, with a focus on technical Software Development Life Cycle related roles based in Auckland.
 

Our approach

We work from a person centered approach, with a genuine interest in connecting people on similar journeys where CQ (Curiosity Quotient) and PQ (Passion Quotient) in most cases wight more than commercial experience.
No two teams are the same. So we become part of the teams we work with to live and breathe the culture, understand the challenges and attract people who can complement and grow with the team, adding value whilst learning from others.
Based on deep collaboration, mutual trust and transparency we work to gain good understanding of the teams we work with and represent, backed up by multiple powerful networks and channels to identify and engage candidates, we aim for the best fit globally when the right person is not based locally.

What can we do for your business?

End to End recruitment

  • Taking up the full cycle from getting involved in workforce planning > creating job descriptions > advertising> handling all applications > shortlisting> organising meetings/interviews > present/ negotiate offers> reference checks> issue & deliver contracts.
  • Fast tracking from requirement to recruitment and post placement.

Onboarding

  • Taking up compliance for new employees> MoJ check, IRD and internal forms> set them up in system.
  • Design and implement an onboarding framework that takes the new employees through company history & culture> internal policies> product/ service training> scheduling meeting with relevant teams/ members> schedule meeting with you and them to set 3, 6 & 12 month goals > introduce them to a Performance Review framework.

Performance Review

  • Discuss & design with you what areas will be measured and how in a performance review > implement a performance review system> onboarding existing employees into this system according to the 3, 6 & 12 months goals set during the onboarding period.
  • Analyse and provide relevant market information to assess individual and team performance, current annual salary package vs market trends.

People Development & Company Benefits

  • Analyse with you horizontal and lateral development opportunities for existing & new employees.
  • Analyse best market practices, identify and implement benefits that fit within the company's vision and budget.
  • Identify and coordinate relevant team events and activities.
  • Seek industry training that can add value to the company at affordable prices.
